Hello, my pet

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2026

Hello, my pet stores all your pet's diaries, memories, and photos entirely on your device. We do not upload, collect, or have access to your personal data or photos.

1. Overview

Hello, my pet ("the App") is a 100-day virtual pet raising game. Your pet's progress, diary entries, captured memories, and photos are stored locally on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

2. On-Device Storage

All gameplay data and memory photos are stored on your device:

  • Pet stats, diary entries, and trophies are saved locally only
  • Memory photos selected from your library are stored on your device
  • Photos and personal data are never uploaded to our servers
  • The developer has no access to your gameplay data or photos

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Analytics & Crash Reports

The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve game quality. The following anonymized data may be collected:

  • App launches, screen views, and session duration
  • Anonymous game events (e.g., action performed, stage transition reached)
  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Crash reports and stack traces (no personally identifiable information)

No personal information, photos, or pet names are included in these reports.

3.2 Advertising Data

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID)
  • IP address (for approximate location-based ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

3.3 Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Photo Library: To select memory photos of your pet (optional, used only when you choose to attach an image)
  • Tracking Transparency: To display personalized advertisements (iOS only)

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or personal identity
  • Contents of your pet's diary or photos (these stay on your device)
  • Contacts, call logs, or messages
  • Precise location data
  • Device storage contents beyond photos you explicitly select

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics): Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Advertising. Google Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

The App is appropriate for ages 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not contain any content inappropriate for children.

7. Data Deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by:

  • Uninstalling the App (removes all local data permanently)
  • Clearing App data through your device settings
  • Starting a new pet from the in-app "New Friend" option (resets the previous pet's data)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

  • フォトライブラリから選択した思い出の写真はデバイス内に保存されます

  • 開発者はゲームデータや写真にアクセスできません

  • トラッキングの透明性: パーソナライズされた広告の表示のため（iOSのみ）

4. 収集しない情報

  • 氏名・メールアドレス・個人の身元情報
  • ペットの日記の内容や写真（デバイス内のみに保管）
  • 連絡先・通話履歴・メッセージ

  • Google AdMob: 広告. Googleプライバシーポリシー

本アプリは4歳以上を対象としており、13歳未満の子供から意図的に個人情報を収集することはありません。子供に不適切なコンテンツは含まれていません。

すべてのデータはデバイス内にローカル保存されるため、以下の方法で削除できます:
