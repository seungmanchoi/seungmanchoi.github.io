BibleLingo

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026

BibleLingo processes all Bible data, searches, and scrapbooks entirely on your device. We do not upload, collect, or have access to your personal study data.

1. Overview

BibleLingo ("the App") is a mobile application for multi-language Bible study. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

2. On-Device Processing

  • Bible database and search operations happen 100% locally
  • Scrapped verses and study progress are saved directly to your device storage
  • The developer has no access to your personal study records

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Advertising Data

The free version uses Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA/GAID) and approximate location for ad targeting.

3.2 Purchase Information

In-app purchases are handled by Apple/Google. We do not store payment info. Verification is done via RevenueCat.

  • Google AdMob: Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Privacy Policy
