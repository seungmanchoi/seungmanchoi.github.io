Lumora: Daily Fortune

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

Lumora is usable without an account. Your birth date, birth time, and gender — used to calculate astrology, Saju (Four Pillars), Chinese zodiac, and tarot readings — are stored only on your device and are never sent to our servers. We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or precise location. The app does show ads through Google AdMob, which may use an advertising identifier as described below.

1. Overview

Lumora: Daily Fortune is a fortune-telling app (Western astrology, Korean Saju, Chinese zodiac, and tarot) developed by Seungman Choi. Readings are generated on your device from pre-written content; there is no fortune-telling backend. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

2. Information Stored On Your Device

The app stores the following locally so it can produce your readings and remember your preferences. This data remains on your device unless a future online feature clearly asks you to submit it:

  • Birth profile: birth date, birth time (optional), and gender — used solely to compute your astrology, Saju, Chinese zodiac, and tarot readings on the device.
  • App state: oracle intimacy progress, daily reading history, language, theme, true-solar-time toggle, and sound settings.

You can erase all of this by deleting the app from your device.

3. Analytics And Diagnostics

The production app may use Firebase Analytics to understand aggregate usage events (such as app opens, screen views, and reading views) and Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash and stability diagnostics. This data is used to improve the app and does not include your birth profile, name, email address, contacts, or precise location.

4. Advertising

Lumora displays ads through Google AdMob (banner, interstitial, and rewarded formats). To serve ads — including personalized ads — AdMob may use a mobile advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android) and related ad-interaction data.

  • iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT): we show Apple's tracking prompt. The advertising identifier is used for personalized ads only if you allow tracking; otherwise you still see non-personalized ads.
  • EEA, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR): we show a Google-certified consent message before ads load and respect your choice.
  • You can change or withdraw your choice anytime, or in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: advertising. See Google Privacy Policy and how Google uses data.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: app usage analytics and crash diagnostics. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: app distribution and store-level diagnostics.

6. Children

Lumora is a general-audience app and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Deletion And Consent Reset

You can remove all local app data — including your birth profile — by deleting the app from your device. You can reset your ad-consent choice from the device tracking settings. Store-level diagnostic records are managed by Apple or Google.

8. Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact blueng.choi@gmail.com.

Lumora はアカウントなしで利用できます。占星術、四柱推命、干支、タロットの鑑定に使用する生年月日、出生時刻、性別は端末内にのみ保存され、当社のサーバーへ送信されることはありません。氏名、メールアドレス、連絡先、写真、正確な位置情報は収集しません。ただし、本アプリは Google AdMob を通じて広告を表示し、以下に説明するとおり広告識別子を使用する場合があります。

Lumora: Daily Fortune は Seungman Choi が開発した占いアプリ(西洋占星術、韓国式四柱推命、干支、タロット)です。鑑定はあらかじめ用意されたコンテンツをもとに端末上で生成され、占い用のサーバーはありません。本ポリシーは、アプリが利用するデータとその取り扱いを説明します。

2. 端末に保存される情報

アプリは鑑定の生成と設定の記憶のため、以下の情報を端末内に保存します。将来のオンライン機能で明確に送信を求めない限り、このデータは端末外へ送信されません。

3. 分析と診断

本番版アプリでは、Firebase Analytics を使用してアプリ起動、画面表示、鑑定の閲覧などの集計イベントを把握し、Firebase Crashlytics を使用してクラッシュおよび安定性の診断情報を受け取る場合があります。このデータはアプリ改善のために使用され、出生情報、氏名、メールアドレス、連絡先、正確な位置情報は含みません。

  • EEA、英国、スイス(GDPR): 広告の読み込み前に Google 認定の同意メッセージを表示し、選択を尊重します。
  • 選択はいつでも変更・撤回でき、iOS の設定 > プライバシーとセキュリティ > トラッキングでも変更できます。
